BIOGRAPHY PAGE

Biography

Hon. Sani Muhammed Sha'aban is a Nigerian based politician, born on the 24th August, 1958, into the family of _______________________ in Zaria formaly known as Zau-zau kingdom, in Kaduna State, Nigeria,

Educational Background:

Graduated with a B.Sc in Accounting from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria. Also attended the London School of Accountancy. Sha'aban further obtained a Certificate in Financial Administration from South West London University.

Politics:

Three-time member of the Nigerian House of Representatives that represented Sabon Gari Zaria up to 2007. Chairman, House of Representatives committee on Pension. Member, of the following House of Representatives committees: Petroleum Resources Science and Technology Public Accounts 2007 Contested governorship of Kaduna State on platform of ANPP and mounted a legal challenge to the election result declared by INEC. This according to Sha'aban is in attempt to see that justice is done to the people of Kaduna State and their vote is respected. Has served meritoriously in various capacities on the board of many corporations. Likewise, 2011, 2014 and 2023.

Party: Hon. Sani Muhammed Sha'aban is a Founding member of "All Nigeria Peoples Party (ANPP) " Kaduna State branch, where he had beautify political adventure and served a two term tenor as a House Member representing Sabon Gari Zaria local government from 2000 - 2007.
Hon. Sani Muhammed Sha'aban joined the All Progressive Congress (APC) as also Founding member in 2011 after a political revolution in nigeria which had many political parties of the same political view marged together to form a stronghood which is the rulling party in nigeria today.
